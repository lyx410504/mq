What should I pay attention to when using
2019.10.27

At present, many consumers have purchased sonic electric toothbrushes, but the use of electric toothbrushes is different from ordinary toothbrushes. Many people are not comfortable with the first use. What should you pay attention to when using sonic electric toothbrush for the first time?

Compared with the traditional manual brushing method, the electric toothbrush is undoubtedly easier and more convenient to use, and the correct use method can bring about better tooth cleaning effect. Pay attention to the following matters during the initial use:

Put the toothbrush in the mouth again:

Before using the sonic electric toothbrush for the first time, make sure that the brush head is tightly inserted into the toothbrush shaft to avoid loosening of the brush head handle during use. Then you can soak the brush head with warm water and then squeeze in the right amount of toothpaste. The thing to note here is to put the brush head into the mouth and start the toothbrush, otherwise it will cause the toothpaste to splash. This is a situation that many first-time users are prone to. .

Use a lower frequency vibration mode:

Since the vibration frequency of the sonic electric toothbrush is many times higher than that of the manual, the oral cavity will not be suitable during the initial use, and the water flow shock caused by the high-frequency vibration will cause the long-filled gingival sulcus to be opened and effectively cleaned, but also It is prone to bleeding gums, which is normal in the first few uses, so it is recommended to use a milder cleaning mode for the first time.
